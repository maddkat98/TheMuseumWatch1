Instructions

You are the museum watch! As a guarder of art, you're job is to protect them.
Tonight, an art thief has managed to break in and has started stealing famous paintings. It's time for you to fufill your duty!

Controls to move are the arrow keys:
Up Arrow Key(up)
Down Arrow Key(down)
Left Arrow Key(left)
Right Arrow Key

A list of the stolen paintings will appear to the
right of the game. Catching the art
thief once will get you a painting back
and will immediately be removed from the list.

The art thief knows a guard is after him, so don't expect him to stay in the museum too long.
Now go on and take those paintings back!
